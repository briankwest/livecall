Refund Policy

Overview:
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all purchases. Customers can request a full refund within 30 days of their purchase date.

Eligibility:
- Purchase must be made within the last 30 days
- Original receipt or order number required
- Product must not be on the excluded items list (see below)

How to Request a Refund:
1. Log into your account at our website
2. Navigate to Order History
3. Click "Request Refund" next to the eligible order
4. Select refund reason and submit request
5. Refund will be processed within 3-5 business days

Excluded Items:
- Digital downloads
- Gift cards
- Clearance items marked "Final Sale"
- Custom or personalized products

Refund Methods:
- Credit card purchases: Refunded to original payment method
- PayPal purchases: Refunded to PayPal account
- Cash purchases: Store credit issued

Processing Time:
- Standard refunds: 3-5 business days
- International refunds: 7-10 business days
- Bank processing may add 2-3 additional days

Contact Support:
For refund assistance, contact our support team:
- Email: refunds@company.com
- Phone: 1-800-REFUNDS
- Live chat available 24/7
